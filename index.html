Q1Quiz1: My Favourite Movie

WALL•E

WALL•E is a 2008 animated romanitc science fiction film produced by Pixar, an animation studio for Walt Disney Pictures. After the making of "Finding Nemo", Andrew Stanton, the director of WALL•E, wanted to create a film set in space. The film has minimal dialogue and is mainly using visuals to communicate the meaning. Waste Allocation Load Lifter Earth-Class or WALL•E, is a robot that collects trash on planet Earth until one day where he boards the ship and meets a new robot.
